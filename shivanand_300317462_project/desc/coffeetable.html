Coffee Tables

A coffee table is a style of low table which is designed to be placed front of or next to sofa or upholstered chairs for convenient support of beverages, magazines, books, decorative objects and other small items to be used while sitting, such as beverages.

Coffee tables are usually found in the living room or sitting room. These coffee tables enhances the beauty of your living room.

We, at Yara, do customized design of your coffee table. The finest hand crafted resin river tables by Yara, made with savaged wood and by using ultra clear UV resistant epoxy resin.

The table tops are made by using natural teak wood and resin poured in layer after layer.

The legs can be made out of clients taste depending on type of material choice(i.e Wood, Metal)

The table has threaded inserts that can be disassembled and assembled easily.
