Dining Tables

Dining room is the room for consuming food. It is the place where the family gather to eat and share their day activities, the place where family unite to share their thoughts and feelings.

These tables are normally adjacent to the kitchen for convenience in serving. These dining tables are designed to be large and have number of chairs according to the family need

We, at Yara, do customized design of these dining table based on the size of client's family and their taste of wood and color. The finest hand crafted resin river tables by Yara, made with savaged wood and by using ultra clear UV resistant epoxy resin.

The table tops are made by using natural teak wood and resin poured in layer after layer.

The legs can be made out of clients taste depending on type of material choice(i.e Wood, Metal)

The table has threaded inserts that can be disassembled and assembled easily.
