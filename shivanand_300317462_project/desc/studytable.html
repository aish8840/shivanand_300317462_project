Bed-side Tables

Bed-side table, alternatively called as night-table or daystand or bedside cabinet, is a small table or cabinet designed to stand beside a bed or elsewhere in the bedroom.

Modern nightstands are usually small bedside tables, often with one or sometimes more drawers or shelves. These are often used to support items that might be useful during the night, such as table lap, alarm clock, reading matter, phone, eyeglasses, a drink, or medication

We, at Yara, do customized design of your these bedside table. The finest hand crafted resin river tables by Yara, made with savaged wood and by using ultra clear UV resistant epoxy resin.

The table tops are made by using natural teak wood and resin poured in layer after layer.

The legs can be made out of clients taste depending on type of material choice(i.e Wood, Metal)

The table has threaded inserts that can be disassembled and assembled easily.
